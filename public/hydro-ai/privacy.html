Hydro AI — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Hydro AI (“the app,” “we,” “us”) helps you track your hydration by taking a photo of a drink and estimating its volume. We built the app to collect as little as possible. This policy explains exactly what we handle and why.

What we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your email address and (for Google) your name and profile photo. We use these to create your account and personalize the app. We never receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Hydration logs. For each drink you log we store the estimated volume, beverage type, a hydration value, a timestamp, the AI confidence score, and a small thumbnail image. This is the data that powers your dashboard, history, and streaks.
  • Profile & preferences. Your display name, daily goal, units (ml/oz), and reminder settings.
  • Accountability circle (optional). If you connect with friends, we store the connection so you can see each other’s daily progress summaries and streaks. Connected friends never see your individual logs.

Photos are processed, not stored

The full-resolution photo you take is sent to our secure analysis service only to estimate the drink, and is discarded immediately — it is never saved on our servers. Only a small thumbnail generated on your device, plus the resulting estimate, is stored in your account.

How the AI works

Drink estimates are produced by Anthropic’s Claude Vision model, called from our own secure backend. The image is used solely to generate your estimate and is not used to train any model.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not store your full-resolution photos.

Where your data lives

Your account data is stored using Supabase (Postgres, Auth, and Storage). Access is restricted by row-level security so you can only read and write your own data.

Deleting your data

You are in control of your data at any time from within the app:

  • Clear all history (Profile → Data & account) removes all your logged drinks while keeping your account.
  • Delete account (Profile → Data & account) permanently deletes your account and all associated data — profile, logs, thumbnails, and connections. This cannot be undone.

If you can’t access the app, email us at strideaicoach@gmail.com and we will delete your account on request.

Exporting your data

You can download a copy of your data at any time from Profile → Data & account → Export my data. It produces a portable file containing your profile and full log history.

Children

Hydro AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email strideaicoach@gmail.com.
